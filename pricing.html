Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a "page"?

Each PDF page processed counts as one page. Multi-page documents will use multiple pages from your monthly allowance.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All new users get 50 free pages to test our service before committing to a paid plan.
